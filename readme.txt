Step 1.Open Google cloud console
Step 2.In the GCP Console, on the Navigation menu (), click APIs & Services.
Step 3.Click Enable APIs and Services.
Step 4.Search for Dialogflow, and then select Dialogflow API.
Step 5.Click Enable.
Step 6.In a new browser tab, go to http://dialogflow.com.
Step 7.Click Go to Console in the upper-right corner.
Step 8.When prompted, click Sign in with Google.
Step 9.Allow Dialogflow to access your Google Account generated from Qwiklabs (ex: gcp...@qwiklabs.net), and accept the terms of service.
Step 10.On the Dialogflow main menu (on the left), click Create Agent.
Step 11.For Agent name enter pizzaBot
Step 12.Create Agent.
Step 13.Click on the settings gear icon next to your agent name, and click the Export and Import tab.
Step 14.Click on IMPORT FROM ZIP and select the zip file from previous lab to import back all the configuration of your agent.
Step 15.Click IMPORT.
Step 16.Then run the bot in right panel
